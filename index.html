EggWalk

Current project - A PC game to be published Spring of 2016. EggWalk is a 3D level based runner game developed by a team of 14 students over a six month period. The player must run through a city delivering eggs to checkpoints while balancing the fragile egg and avoiding obstacles. Developed in the Unity game engine.

Lights Out

Developed a game playable on iOS devices with touch screen controls. Features a top down procedurally generated maze the player must run through while collecting light. Built over the course of a semester with a team of 5.

HHH

Developed a touch screen game for Android devices and published to the Google Play store. The game is based on building a homeless shelter and managing resources to help supply the visitors with food, healthcare, or clean clothes. Used 2D isometric sprites for tile based gameplay, allowing placement of resources and upgrades. Worked on a team of 8 throughout the course of a semester.
